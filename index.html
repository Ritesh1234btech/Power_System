Transmission Losses (B-coefficients)

Include transmission losses in dispatch (Kron's loss formula: P_L = ΣΣ Pi·Bij·Pj)

B-matrix auto-generates when losses are enabled (or use benchmark preset's matrix). Diagonal terms dominate; off-diagonal terms are typically small.

Valve-Point Loading Effect

Add valve-point ripple: C(P) = aP²+bP+c + |e·sin(f·(Pmin−P))|

This term makes the cost surface non-convex (multiple local minima) — the reason plain λ-iteration can get stuck, and why population-based metaheuristics are used instead.

Prohibited Operating Zones

Bands of output a unit cannot run in continuously (e.g. shaft/vibration resonance). Format per generator: lo1-hi1;lo2-hi2

Ramp Rate Limits

Max MW a unit can change between consecutive periods — used in the Multi-Period tab. Up/down limits in MW per period.
